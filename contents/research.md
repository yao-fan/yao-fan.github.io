#### Areas

**Areas of specialization:** Philosophy of AI; Social Epistemology; Theories of Justice; Game Theory; Decision Theory; Philosophy of Economics; Logic.

**Areas of competence:** Philosophy of Science; History of Modern Philosophy; Political Philosophy; Ethics; Chinese Philosophy.

#### Current Work

My current research focuses on social and political epistemic systems: how people rely on one another, how institutions identify expertise, and how strategic incentives can affect collective belief and decision making.

Several projects connect this work to AI and artificial social intelligence, especially questions about epistemic deference, decision support, explanation, and institutional governance in environments where human and artificial agents interact.
